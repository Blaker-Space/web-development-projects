BlakerSpace's Coding Coffee

Description

BlakerSpace's Coding Coffee recipe is any developer's key to heightened efficiency.

All you need is a pour-over coffee maker, some hot water, and some coffee grounds. It takes 69 seconds to make.

Ingredients

  • Pour-over coffee maker
  • A coffee filter
  • Coffee grounds
  • Hot water

Steps

  1. Chuck a filter in that pour-over coffee maker unless you want straight grounds and water
  2. Put coffee grounds in the filter, NOT IN THE HOT WATER
  3. Pour the hot water over the grounds in the filter
  4. When the water level gets high, let the filter drain until continuing to pour
  5. Stop pouring when you either run out of water or have enough coffee
