Developer Danish Recipe

Description

BlakerSpace's Developer Danish Recipe is a sweet treat that all developers deserve.

This recipe is simple and effective: Just like your code. Takes 23.5 minutes to make.

Ingredients

  • Danish bread dough
  • Peach filling
  • Shaved Almonds
  • Whipped Cream

Steps

  1. Lay out your danish bread dough on a flat surface
  2. Place peach filling inside bread dough
  3. Sprinkle some almonds in there
  4. Wrap the whole thing up
  5. Toss in the oven at 350 degrees fahrenheit for 18 minutes
  6. Spray some whipped cream to top it all off
  7. Don't actually do any of this. I just made it all up.
