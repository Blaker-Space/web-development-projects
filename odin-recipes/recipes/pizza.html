BlakerSpace's Programming Pizza

Description

BlakerSpace's Programming Pizza is a delectable dish that is sure to level-up your programming ten-fold.

This pizza is made using traditional pizza dough, marinara sauce, feta cheese, and spinach. Cook time is 15 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 Pizza bread dough
  • 3 Tbsp marinara sauce
  • 4 oz. Feta cheese
  • 2 oz. Spinach

Steps

  1. Spread the pizza dough into a circular shape making sure to include some tosses in the air
  2. Throw your 3 tablespoons of marinara on that bad boy. Sauce it all up.
  3. Cover with copious amounts of Feta cheese. This is the key ingredient in speeding up your programming.
  4. Top it all off with some Spinach so that you too can be strong to the finish.
  5. Chuck that hunk of deliciousness in the oven for like 10 mins or so on 420 degrees fahrenheit
