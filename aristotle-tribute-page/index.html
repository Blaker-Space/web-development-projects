Who Is Aristotle?

Aristotle was a Greek Philosopher known for pioneering discoveries in Philosophy and Science. After being educated by Plato, Aristotle was known for tutoring Alexander The Great: The King of the ancient Greek Kingdom of Macedon.

Aristotle's Life:

  • 384 BCE: Aristotle is born in Stagira
  • 367 BCE: Aristotle begins studying at Plato's Academy
  • 342 BCE: Aristotle tutors Alexander the Great
  • 325 BCE: Aristotle writes his book "On the Heavens"
  • 322 BCE: Death of Aristotle

Memorable Quotes:

"It is the mark of an educated mind to be able to entertain a thought without accepting it..."

"Knowing yourself is the beginning of all wisdom..."
"Educating the mind without educating the heart is no education at all..."
"What is a friend? A single soul dwelling in two bodies..."
